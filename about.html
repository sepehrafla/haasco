Our History

HAAS Heating and Air Conditioning System Co. (HAAS) was found by a coterie of well experienced and educated engineers and technicians with a goal to achieve the highest levels of perfection and excellence in the HVAC projects, a professional company with a core engineering focus on sustainabiltiy and quality service and maintenance of Heating, Air conditioning, Refrigeration, Ventilation and water treatment projects. HAAS Heating and Air Conditioning System strive for on-time delivery schedules. Recognised for these qualities, our company is marching ahead with an eye towards a bright future. HAAS Heating and Air Conditioning System can supply several equipment mainly from world well known companies with totally satisfaction of customers with high quality and lower price and shorter delivery time.

Our Mission :

Streamline the customer satisfaction base, strengthen internal operating process and establish pro-active planning mechanism to accelerate the transition to sustainable energy in Canada. With over two decades of experience providing quality HVAC service and over 5 years of advising home owners with best energy efficiency solution, we help customers save cost while transition their energy consumption towards sustainable sources.

Our Vision :

is to become “customers’ preferred Company” in providing end to end sustainable transition solution. We strive for excelence in design, Engineering, Installation, Maintanance and support for Heating, Air conditioning, Refrigeration, Ventilation and water treatment projects with zero defects within shortest lead time. Our core competencies in our field of activities are being continuously improved to be best-in-class.
